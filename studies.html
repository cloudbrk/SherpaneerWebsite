Streamlining A Fragmented & Heavily Customized Salesforce Implementation

Our client was a sales development consulting firm that specializes in helping a wide range of high-tech clients accelerate new business growth. They work to give their clients a competitive advantage in the reach and effectiveness of their sales development efforts.

The Sherpaneer Result

We partnered with our client to perform a Salesforce Health Check on an existing Sales implementation. The Org had become fragmented from the use of multiple admins, changes in key stakeholders and an over reliance on platform customizations without any oversight. On assessment we found that over 60% of the customization could be refactored to configuration and/or removed from the system with the remaining code standardized by using our trusted coding frameworks. We also learned that their user adoption was low and that there was a bunch of manual data entry for automated features. We delivered these changes without impact to the business users, completely streamlining the administration overhead of the Org. We also provided a flexible application support model for delivery of on-going support and enhancement requests as they built their internal talent and Salesforce support organization.

"Sherpaneer’s seasoned consulting team helped streamline and simplify our Salesforce experience with their Heath Check service. We were also able to leverage their Success & Support model when we needed to augment memoryBlue’s internal Salesforce team in a seamless fashion at a critical time in our business, when we were experiencing high growth."

- Rohit Amba, Chief Operations Officer, memoryBlue

Enhancing Salesforce Administration Through Strategic Staff Augmentation

Our client faced staffing challenges and lacked trust in its Salesforce Ecosytem.

The Sherpaneer Result

We partnered with our client and proposed a strategic staff augmentation approach, deploying a highly skilled, certified, and knowledgeable Salesforce administrator to collaborate with our client's internal team. This approach aimed to fill the gap in their department, providing immediate relief while fostering long-term benefits such as faster issue resolution, efficient feature enhancements, and adherence to industry standards. The addition of the skilled Salesforce administrator resulted in improved user satisfaction, accelerated adoption of Salesforce features, and a more dynamic and user-friendly Salesforce environment. Overall, the strategic staff augmentation approach proved effective in overcoming Salesforce administration hurdles and driving long-term success for the client's organization.

"Sherpaneer has helped me close the knowledge gap in taking over the team responsible for Salesforce Support. They are to the point, action oriented, and knowledgeable. If you are struggling with where to start and what to prioritize, they will help you get aligned and start to move forward with a plan to match your budget, technology status, and people skills."

- Sohil Sami, Senior Vice President, ScienceLogic
