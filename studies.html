Streamlining a fragmented & heavily customized Salesforce Implementation

Our client was a sales development consulting firm that specializes in helping a wide range of high-tech clients accelerate new business growth. They work to give their clients a competitive advantage in the reach and effectiveness of their sales development efforts.

The Sherpaneer Result

We partnered with our client to perform a Salesforce Health Check on an existing Sales implementation. The Org had become fragmented from the use of multiple admins, changes in key stakeholders and an over reliance on platform customizations without any oversight. On assessment we found that over 60% of the customization could be refactored to configuration and/or removed from the system with the remaining code standardized by using our trusted coding frameworks. We also learned that their user adoption was low and that there was a bunch of manual data entry for automated features. We delivered these changes without impact to the business users, completely streamlining the administration overhead of the Org. We also provided a flexible application support model for delivery of on-going support and enhancement requests as they built their internal talent and Salesforce support organization.
