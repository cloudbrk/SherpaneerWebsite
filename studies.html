Streamlining a Fragmented & Heavily Customized Salesforce Implementation

The Problem

Our client was a sales development consulting firm that specializes in helping a wide range of high-tech clients accelerate new business growth. They work to give their clients a competitive advantage in the reach and effectiveness of their sales development efforts. Additionally, their Salesforce Organization had become fragmented from the use of multiple admins, changes in key stakeholders, and an over reliance on platform customizations without any oversight.

The Solution

Our Health Check identified over 60% of our client's customizations could be refactored or completely removed from the system alongside standardizing the existing code by using trusted frameworks. Additionally, user adoption was low and many users were performing manual data entry where processes could be automated. We delivered these changes without negative impact to the business users, completely streamlining the administration overhead. We also provided a flexible application support model for delivery of on-going enhancement requests as a Salesforce Support Organization was built out.

"Sherpaneer’s seasoned consulting team helped streamline and simplify our Salesforce experience with their Heath Check service. We were also able to leverage their Success & Support model when we needed to augment memoryBlue’s internal Salesforce team in a seamless fashion at a critical time in our business, when we were experiencing high growth."

- Rohit Amba, Chief Operations Officer, memoryBlue

Enhancing Salesforce Administration Through Strategic Staff Augmentation

The Problem

Our client faced staffing challenges and lacked trust in its Salesforce Ecosystem. Departmental gaps were identified in areas of issue resolution, efficient feature enhancements, and adherence to industry standards.

The Solution

We partnered with our client and proposed a strategic staff augmentation approach, deploying a highly skilled, certified, and knowledgeable Salesforce administrator to collaborate with our client's internal team. The addition of the skilled Salesforce administrator resulted in improved user satisfaction, accelerated adoption of Salesforce features, and created a more dynamic and user-friendly Salesforce environment. Overall, the strategic staff augmentation approach proved effective in overcoming hurdles and driving long-term success for the client's organization.

"Sherpaneer has helped me close the knowledge gap in taking over the team responsible for Salesforce Support. They are to the point, action oriented, and knowledgeable. If you are struggling with where to start and what to prioritize, they will help you get aligned and start to move forward with a plan to match your budget, technology status, and people skills."

- Sohil Sami, Senior Vice President, ScienceLogic
