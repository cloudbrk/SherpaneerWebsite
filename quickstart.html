Quick Start

Sherpaneer has designed Quick Start as a dedicated, efficient and cost effective way for clients to get their projects underway. A Quickstart is a limited engagement implementation that focuses on key planning and decision making, standard and custom configurations and essential training for companies who want to start working with Salesforce as quickly as possible. Quick start packages are great for individual departments or small sales & customer services teams who want to get the most out of their Salesforce experience from day one.

Our experience shows that the work we do with clients early on has the greatest impact on its success - if you are undecided on whether you want us to help you over the full length of a project, you can ask us just to help you get your team up to speed and prepared. Although we would never advocate a “quick-fix” approach to business, a streamlined service that utilizes Sherpaneer’s experience can more efficiently improve your businesses where time and money are of the essence.

Our Quick Start projects consist of focused workshops and meetings, which allows us to be flexible and efficiently work around your team’s availability.

Marketing Cloud

This quick start aims to rapidly deploy and configure essential elements, providing a foundation for effective marketing operations using Salesforce. The focus is on quickly initiating campaigns and automation to drive early results. A quick start for a Salesforce Marketing package typically includes:

  1. Objective Definition: Clearly define marketing objectives and align them with organizational goals.
  2. Campaign Setup: Configure and set up initial marketing campaigns to kickstart engagement and lead generation.
  3. Email Marketing Automation: Implement basic email marketing automation for targeted and personalized communication.
  4. Data Segmentation: Establish initial data segmentation to ensure targeted messaging based on audience characteristics.
  5. User Training (One Hour): Conduct a concise training session covering key features of Salesforce Marketing for users involved in campaign management.

Sales Cloud

This quick start aims to swiftly implement key elements, providing a foundation for effective sales operations using Salesforce Sales Cloud. A quick start for a Salesforce Sales Cloud package typically includes:

  1. Objective Overview: Understand and outline specific sales objectives to tailor the implementation.
  2. Customization for Sales Processes: Configure and customize Sales Cloud to align with the organization's unique sales processes.
  3. Lead and Opportunity Management: Set up lead and opportunity management for efficient tracking and conversion.
  4. User Roles and Permissions: Define user roles and permissions to ensure secure access and data integrity.
  5. User Training (One Hour): Conduct a focused training session covering essential features, reports, and dashboards.

Data Cloud

Embark on a data-driven transformation with our Salesforce Data Cloud Quick Start. Tailored for businesses ready to harness the power of data, this solution offers a rapid and effective launchpad into the world of Salesforce Data Cloud. A quick start for a Salesforce Data Cloud package typically includes:

  1. Connect & Enrich: Integrate Data Cloud seamlessly with your existing ecosystem to instantly enrich your data.
  2. Map with Precision: Tailor data mapping to your specific needs, ensuring accurate and relevant insights.
  3. Empower with Insights: Get real-time access to data insights, driving faster, more informed decisions.
  4. Prioritize Quality: Implement essential data governance measures to maintain data quality and compliance.
  5. User Training (One Hour): Access personalized training to equip your team with the skills to navigate and utilize Data Cloud effectively.

Revenue Cloud

This quick start is designed to swiftly deploy and configure essential elements, particularly beneficial for onboarding one business unit or product line onto the Salesforce Revenue Cloud/CPQ tool. A quick start for a Salesforce Revenue Cloud/CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) package typically focuses on:

  1. Objective Definition: Clearly outline revenue and CPQ goals, tailoring the implementation for specific business units or product lines.
  2. Standard Product Configuration: Configure standard out-of-the-box products with a maximum of three, simplifying quoting processes.
  3. Quote Automation: Implement automated workflows for efficient quote generation and approval.
  4. Contract Management: Establish processes for contract management to ensure accuracy and compliance in agreements.
  5. User Training (One Hour): Conduct a concise training session covering key features of Revenue Cloud/CPQ, tailored for users involved in quoting and pricing processes.

Service Cloud

This quick start is designed to swiftly deploy essential components, optimizing customer service operations through Salesforce Service Cloud. A quick start for a Salesforce Service Cloud package typically includes:

  1. Objective Alignment: Clearly define service objectives and align them with the organization's goals.
  2. Case Management Setup: Configure case management processes to streamline issue resolution and customer support.
  3. Agent Productivity Tools: Implement tools and features to enhance agent productivity, such as knowledge base integration and macros.
  4. Automation for Efficiency: Set up automation rules for routing and prioritizing cases, ensuring efficient resolution.
  5. User Training (One Hour): Conduct a focused training session covering key Service Cloud features, case handling, and productivity tools.

Experience Cloud

This quick start aims to efficiently deploy and configure key elements, ensuring a robust foundation for delivering an enhanced customer experience through Salesforce Customer Experience Cloud. A quick start for a Salesforce Customer Experience Cloud package typically includes:

  1. Objective Definition: Clearly define and understand the specific customer experience objectives to shape the implementation.
  2. Portal Setup: Customize the Experience Cloud Portal using standard templates, incorporating relevant tabs, components, and audience settings.
  3. Security Configuration: Establish a secure environment by configuring Role Hierarchy and Profiles to control data access.
  4. User Training (One Hour): Conduct a concise training session covering administration, moderation, and user setup within the Experience Cloud.
